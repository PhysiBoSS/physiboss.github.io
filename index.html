PhysiBoSS is a multiscale modeling framework integrating Boolean intracellular models into the PhysiCell agent-based framework.
PhysiBoSS project is a collaboration between the Computational Systems Biology of Cancer team at Institut Curie and the Computational Biology group at BSC.







Tutorial

We recently released a tutorial on PhysiBoSS.
You can find the preprint on arvix: https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.18371
A step-by-step tutorial is provided as a Supplementary Material.
You can find it, together with all models at: https://physiboss.github.io/tutorial
